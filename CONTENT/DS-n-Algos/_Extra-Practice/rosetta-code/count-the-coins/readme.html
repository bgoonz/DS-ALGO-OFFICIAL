There are four types of common coins in US currency:

  • quarters (25 cents)
  • dimes (10 cents)
  • nickels (5 cents), and
  • pennies (1 cent)

There are six ways to make change for 15 cents:

  • A dime and a nickel
  • A dime and 5 pennies
  • 3 nickels
  • 2 nickels and 5 pennies
  • A nickel and 10 pennies
  • 15 pennies
